How it works

With SPC-Repository, everyone can learn to do data analysis in R, SAS, STATA, MPlus, MATLAB, etc., with ease and a smile.

Analysis Types

 All conceivable statistical analyses are grouped under these major themes: reading and managing data, univariate (one variable analysis), bivariate (two variable analysis), multivariate (multiple variable analysis), other advanced analyses, and graphs

Analysis Techniques

 Select from a wide range of varieties of statistical analysis techniques such as frequency distribution of a variable, mean and many more that you want perform on any given variable.
